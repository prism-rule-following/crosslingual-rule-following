# experimental

Prototypes and in-progress ideas — not yet validated, not relied on by confirmatory results. Anything here should either graduate into [`../canonical/`](../canonical/) once it holds up on held-out data, or be discarded.

## Sub-folders

Mirrors `canonical/`'s structure so prototypes can be developed in place before promotion:

- `data/` — experimental rule categories, generator variants, translation approaches.
- `models/` — new model wrappers or hook strategies under evaluation.
- `evaluation/` — candidate checker functions or judge prompts not yet calibrated.
- `probing/` — new classifier architectures or probing strategies.
- `causal/` — alternative localization/patching approaches under test.
- `transport/` — new transformation levels or routing heuristics.
- `analysis/` — exploratory analyses and hypothesis generation (§6.2).
- `configs/` — configs for experimental runs.
- `notebooks/` — scratch/exploratory notebooks.
- `results/` — outputs from experimental runs, not treated as final evidence.
